Biography

Kai Wang is a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Sebastien Gros at the Department of Engineering Cybernetics of Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Norway. He received his B.E. in Space Information and Digital Technology from Xidian University in 2017, and M.E. under the supervision of Prof. Boris Houska in Computer Science from Shanghaitech University in 2020. His research interest centers in the field of risk-based control, inculding optimization- and learning-based control algorithms design.

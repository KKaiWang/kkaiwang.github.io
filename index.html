Biography

Kai Wang is a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Aeronautical and Automotive Engineering at Loughborough University. His research focuses on model predictive control, as well as robust and risk-based decision-making under uncertainty.

He received his B.E. in Space Information and Digital Technology from Xidian University in 2017, and M.E. in Computer Science from Shanghaitech University in 2020. From 2020 to 2024, he completed his Ph.D. from the Department of Cybernetics Engineeering at Norweigian University of Science and Technology (NTNU).
